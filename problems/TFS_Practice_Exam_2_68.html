So the brake horsepower, that's the mechanical power of a spinning shaft that's driving the pump, is more than the water horsepower. How much more, depends on the mechanical efficiency of the pump and the efficiency of the pump was given as 84%. So the water horsepower is 15 horsepower and that's being divided by 0.84.

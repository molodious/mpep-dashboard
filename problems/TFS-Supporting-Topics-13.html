Q: In the TFS reference handbook equation for evaporative cooler effectiveness, why is the 100 neglected in the solution even though it appears in the equation?
A: The 100 isn't an error—it's there to convert the result to a percentage. If you calculate effectiveness as 0.6 without the 100, multiplying by 100 gives you 60% effectiveness. The handbook includes it for convenience so you don't forget to convert to percentage form.

Hexane C6H14 is combusted with a stoichiometric quantity of dry air at atmospheric pressure. What is the dew point of the product gas? So first of all, what is the dew point in general? The dew point is the temperature at which when you cool air or you cool a mixture of gases, the water vapor that's present in that mixture will condense and fall out of the mixture. So it depends on how much water vapor is in the mixture. And we can find that out by analyzing the combustion reaction and figuring out what the mole fraction of water vapor is and then finding the partial pressure of water vapor and then using the steam table to figure out the dew point.

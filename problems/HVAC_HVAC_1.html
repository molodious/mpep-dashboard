Problem Statement
5000cfm of 76°F air with 60% RH is returned to an air handling unit with a total capacity of 15tons and a design sensible heat ratio of 0.7. The cooling coil is supplied with 43°F chilled water which leaves the coil at 54°F. The supply air conditions are 58°F dry bulb and 56°F wet bulb. What percentage of the sensible cooling capacity is available as spare?
Answer Choices
A.23%
B.46%
C.54%
D.77%

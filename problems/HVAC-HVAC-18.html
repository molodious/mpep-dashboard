Q: I have three related questions about heating degree days: how to apply them in HVAC-18 and 21, why you can't plug HDD directly into the formula Q = U × A × HDD × 24, and where to find these formulas in the reference handbook.
A: The key insight is that the HDD formula requires the ΔT to match the base temperature used to calculate the degree days — plugging in raw HDD without checking the baseline will give you the wrong result.

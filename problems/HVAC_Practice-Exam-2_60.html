A single phase 240 volt motor drives a pump that supplies a hundred pounds per minute of 80 degree water and adds 400 feet of pressure head. The pump is 70% efficient and the motor is 90% efficient. What is the current drawn by the motor? So if we're going to be asked to find the current drawn by the motor and we know the voltage then it would be a good start to try to find the power that's going to be supplied to the motor in order to drive the pump in order to produce some amount of hydraulic horsepower which we have a pretty good chance of being able to do because we've got the feet of head that's added by the pump and we don't have the volume flow rate but we have the mass flow rate and it's water so we know the density of water which should be able to do that conversion.

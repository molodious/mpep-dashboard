Problem Statement
An outside air handling unit delivers 5000cfm of outside air. On a winter day, the outside air is 18◦F and is heated to 68◦F using a hot water coil. Hot water is supplied at 165◦F and returned at 140◦F. What is the required (cid:29)ow rate of hot water?

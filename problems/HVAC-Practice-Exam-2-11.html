Problem Statement
An air handling unit uses 10% outside air at 88◦F and 60% RH and 90% recirculated air returned from the space, which is maintained at 76◦F and 50% RH. What is the dew point temperature of the air entering the coil?
Answer Choices
A 56◦F
B 58◦F
C 63◦F
D 65◦F
Solution
Answer: B

90% recirculated air returned from the space, which is maintained at 76◦F and 50% RH.

What is the dew point temperature of the air entering the coil?

The question does not concern the supply/discharge air after the coil.

Use the Psychrometric Chart to look up the humidity ratio for State 1 and State 2 which are fully defned.

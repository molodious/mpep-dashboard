Since the problem is asking for annual savings, this solution treats costs as negative and revenues as positive.

For year 0 there is a payment for the original purchase of -$100K.

For years 1 through 12 there is an annualized cost of $1K per month which equals $12K per year and a savings of $30K per year for a net annual savings of $18K per year.

For year 12 there is a positive cash fow of $40K for the salvage value.

Write an expression for the annualized savings.

Only the initial cost and salvage value need to be transformed to annualized fgures.

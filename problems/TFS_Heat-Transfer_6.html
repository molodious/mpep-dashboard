⭐ Key Takeaways:
• The key for the log-me temperature difference is to understand the distinction between a parallel flow and a counter flow heat exchanger.
• And that's really important because if you look in the reference handbook under log-me temperature difference or LMTD, you'll find a couple of equations there, but they like to work with the individual temperatures.
• In a parallel flow heat exchanger, the temperatures start out far away from one another and they approach but never reach.
• So the equation then is delta T log mean, or you can write LMT D if you prefer, is the difference between delta TA and delta TB divided by the natural log of the ratio of delta T A to delta TB.
• You may choose to go the same route or you may choose to use the formulas in the reference handbook.

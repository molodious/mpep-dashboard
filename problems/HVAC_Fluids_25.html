Fluids 25. A 40GPM cold water booster pump is located 15 feet below the top of a storage tank held at atmospheric pressure from which water is supplied. The suction piping is made up of 50 feet equivalent length of 2 inch schedule 40 steel. The stored water is never warmer than 60 degrees. What is the net positive suction head available? So, net positive suction head available is essentially the cumulative amount of height of a water column that's sitting on the suction side of a pump. There's always some minimum net positive suction head required which is given by the manufacturer of that pump. And if the net positive suction head is less than what's required, then the pump will cavity and that will damage the impeller. That's what we want to avoid.

• The stored water is never warmer than 60 degrees.

Problem Statement
A pumping system delivers water to a factory through a standard weight steel piping supply line (surface roughness C = 120) with 3 outlets delivering 100gpm to each outlet. The main pipe initially has a 5in nominal diameter, reducing to 4in and 3in after each branch outlet. The (cid:28)rst outlet is located 50ft from the pumping station; the second outlet is 100ft downstream of the (cid:28)rst, and the third outlet is 100ft downstream of the second. What is the pressure loss for the system? Ignore minor losses.
Answer Choices
A.7ft
B.10ft
C.14ft
D.20ft

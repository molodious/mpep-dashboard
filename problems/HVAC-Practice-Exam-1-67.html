Problem Statement
An energy recovery device is used to pre-cool 5000cfm of outside air at 95◦F db / 75◦F wb. An equivalent volume of return air enters the device at 76◦F and 50% relative humidity. After passing through the device the exhaust air is discharged outside at 84◦F db / 70◦F wb. What is the reduction in cooling demand provided by the energy recovery wheel?
